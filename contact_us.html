FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQs

Can I give the cleaner a key?

There are no lock in contracts! You can stop the service at anytime.

Do I get the same cleaner every time?

Payment is due upon completion of the clean and is payable via cash or EFT. The recommended payment option is cash unless the cleaner has a preference for EFT. Payment is to made directly to your cleaner after each service, not to Inner West Domestics.

Do I need to be home when the cleaner comes?

For the initial clean you may want to meet your cleaning tech however this is not necessary. If you are not home you can leave a key so your cleaner can start cleaning upon arrival. Once the cleaning is finished the key will be left in a safe predetermined location that was communicated prior.

Do I need to provide cleaning products?

Yes it is better to use your cleaning equipment and products in your home. For Spring Clean services the cleaner will bring all necessary equipment and products.
